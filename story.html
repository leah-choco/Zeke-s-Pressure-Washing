Our Story

Juan Choco

Juan was born and raised in the nation of Belize in Central America. As a young adult. he came to study in Dallas, Texas at a Christian university. There he met his wife, Leah, and after marriage, they relocated to Middle Tennessee to raise their family. Juan's background is in construction, roofing, and pressure washing. He brings all of his life experience and expertise to every job he encounters.

Zeke's Pressure Washing is named after Juan's oldest son. Starting this business has been a dream come true for the Choco family. We are honored to provide quality services to our customers. This business exists to bless our community. It would be an honor to serve you.
